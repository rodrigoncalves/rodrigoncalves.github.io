Plug-in PAdES for Adobe Reader Free

From 2020, users will be able to verify and generate digital signatures in the Brazilian Public Key Infrastructure (ICP-Brasil) standard in PDF format - the most used tool for visualization and creation of digital documents. Just install the PAdES plugin (extension module), a free solution developed in partnership between the National Institute of Information Technology - ITI and the University of Brasília - UnB. The project was developed in C/C++ with OpenSSL as cryptographic library.
